The creators of Sobriety Meet are Myles Dickens, Leah McClintock-Shapiro, Steven N, and Jess Yeganeh. This is the website that accompanies their final UX/UI Design project.



The team was inspired to create this prototype by hearing the needs of their friends, loved ones, and community, for a comprehensive sober meeting guide that spanned across Alchoholics Anonymous, Narcotics Anonymous, and Secular/Non-Affiliated meetings.

Currently there are over 1,000 meetings in the Minneapolis and St. Paul Metro Area. Navigating that many meetings and finding one that works with an individuals recovery is without doubt daunting.



Our goal was to make that journey a little easier for our user. By providing filters, an interactive map, inabiling the ability to favorite and see saved meetings on a personalized calendar, Sobriety Meet offers a visual guide for finding and coordinating meetings.
